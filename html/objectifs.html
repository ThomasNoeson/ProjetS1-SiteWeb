Objectifs des stages

This internship is mandatory. He put into practice a theoretical knowledge. The intern must be implementing solutions for solve a problem given by the company. His work is similar to an employee's work.

The student participates to an intership that him allow to:

  • Acquire responsability
  • Learn to know the work world
  • Develop his professional network
  • Be independent, rigorous
  • Acquire experience
  • Develop other competence
  • Gain confidence, maturity
